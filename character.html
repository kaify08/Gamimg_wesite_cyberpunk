Characters

V - The Mercenary

V grew up in the American wilderness, having been part of the Bakkers nomad family. At the age of 16, they made their first kill when Raffen Shiv scouts assaulted their camp, with V killing one of them with a single shot to the eye.

Johnny Silverhand

John enlisted in the military as a teenager after lying about his real age, and it didn't take long for him to see action. He was sent to Nicaragua to participate in the Second Central American War of 2003.

Judy Álvarez

Judy Álvarez is a skilled braindance techie and a member of the Mox. She has her own apartment in northeastern Kabuki, Watson. Judy is a possible romance option for V in Cyberpunk 2077.
